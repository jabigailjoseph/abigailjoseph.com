## About

**Abigail Joseph** is a data analyst and aspiring biostatistician. She completed her Bachelor of Science in Data Science and Analytics with a minor in Artificial Intelligence at Florida Atlantic University, and she is currently pursuing her Master of Public Health in Biostatistics at Florida International University. She is also a current participant in the FIU-CGR Genomic Scholarship research program.

Abigail is passionate about statistics, professional development, soft skills, and making AI tools and education more accessible and less daunting for individuals with non-technical backgrounds.

When she's not in a study session or research lab, Abigail can be found with her cat. If not, she is either reading, writing, playing The Sims FreePlay, cooking, or nurturing her mango trees and other plants.
